ABOUT ACES

What is ACES?
Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) is a youth-led organization, a collaborative effort of Computer Engineering Students of IOE Purwanchal Campus, Dharan established with an aim to enhance different IT related skills in Technology Enthusiasts. ACES has been providing different workshops and training programs to various students about Computer Technology since 2070 B.S. The physical office lies within the premises of Purwanchal Campus.
We are 9th Executive Committee of ACES.
What Does ACES Do?
ACES hosts programs and organize trainings and workshops related to Computer Engineering field for holistic growth of an individual as Web Developer, Ethical Hacker, SEO personnel, Graphics Designer, a Software Developer and many more. We believe every individual has some inner inertia to become one of best professionals mentioned above. We push those individuals to one step forward and help them to grab the upcoming opportunities using our network.
